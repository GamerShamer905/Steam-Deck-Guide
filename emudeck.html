Emudeck

Emudeck is a software allowing you to emulate tons of old consoles and games directly on your Steam Deck

Step 1

Switch to Desktop Mode.

Step 2

Download the Emudeck Installer

Step 3

Copy the Installer to your Desktop

Step 4

Run the installer

Step 5

Copy your games to the Emulation/roms folder. (Use the Cheat Sheet to find out what folders to put your games in)

Congrats! You have Emudeck Installed Now.

I would show you how to acquire roms, but due to legal complications, I cannot, although I would recommend checking out FMHY as a starting point.
