Accessories

Accessories are an essential part of your Steam Deck experience allowing you to enhance your experience. The four main accessories I would recommend would be a case, thumb grips, an SD Card, and a powerbank.

I find a case essential and the main one I would recommend would be the DBrand Killswitch, but if you are in a country that they don't deliver use this list

I like the default Steam Joystick but for some people they are a pain, so I would recommend the Skull & Co or DBrand Grips, if you can't get those, a simple amazon search should get your far enough.

I love SD Cards, and almost any Sandisk Extreme (Pro as well) or Samsung Pro/Evo works, but if you want a definitive list go here

Powerbanks are also amazing, and there are tons of choices here but really any powerbank over 20000 mAh and that has at least a 100W output is good enough
