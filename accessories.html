Accessories References

Accessories

Case
A case is almost required to protect your Steam Deck. I recommend looking through this list, but would personally recommend the DBrand Killswitch, if they ship to you.
Thumb Grips
Thumb Grips are great for more intense gameplay, most people would tell you a specific brand, but for this one, any one on Amazon will do, if you want a specifc one if you cannot make up your mind, always go for DBrand or Skull&Co
SD Card
A fast SD card is essential, unless you want slow boot times for games. I recommend Sandisk Extreme or Samsung Pro/Evo. For more options, check this list.
Powerbank
A powerbank is great for extended gaming sessions. Any powerbank over 20000 mAh and with a 100W output should work fine. For suggestions, visit here.
