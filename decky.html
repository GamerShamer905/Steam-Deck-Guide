Decky

Decky is a powerful tool used to add plugins

There are 1000+ plugins on Decky for you to explore so I will not be going over the plugins, but rather how to install Decky

  1. Go Into Desktop Mode
  2. Open Your Web Browser and download the installer from decky.xyz or click this link for a direct download.
  3. Drag the file to your Desktop and remove the .download.
  4. Double Click and Execute the File.
  5. Go Back to Gaming Mode.
  6. Click on the Button with 3 Dots.
  7. Click on the New Plug Symbol at the Bottom.
  8. Click on the marketplace icon in the top right.

Congrats, you have Decky installed!

There are many plugins but my favourites are Animation Changer, Full Suspend, HLTB for Deck and SteamGridDB
