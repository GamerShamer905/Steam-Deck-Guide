Decky

Decky is a powerful tool used to add plugins

There are 1000+ plugins on Decky for you to explore so I will not be going over the plugins, but rather how to install Decky

Decky Loader Installation Guide

Step 1

Switch to Desktop Mode.

Step 2

Open your browser and download the installer from decky.xyz or click this direct link.

Step 3

Drag the file to your Desktop and remove the .download extension if present.

Step 4

Double-click the file to execute it.

Step 5

Switch back to Gaming Mode.

Step 6

Click on the button with 3 dots.

Step 7

Click on the new plug icon at the bottom of the screen.

Step 8

Click on the Marketplace icon in the top right corner.

Congrats! You have Decky Installed Now.

There are many plugins but my favourites are Animation Changer, Full Suspend, HLTB for Deck and SteamGridDB
